3. What data do our apps collect?

In short: Our apps do not collect any personal data.

All features work completely locally on your device:

  • Camera: Used only for live display. No images are stored or transmitted without your consent.
  • Photos: Images are stored exclusively in your local photo library.
  • AI Features: All AI analysis runs entirely on your device (Core ML). No images leave your device.
  • OCR & Translation: Uses iOS native APIs and processes data locally.

4. No data transfer to third parties

Our apps do not transfer data to external servers, analytics services, or advertising providers. There are:

  • No tracking tools (like Google Analytics or Firebase)
  • No advertising
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud synchronization

5. In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive any personal payment data from Apple.

6. Permissions

Our apps may require the following permissions:

  • Camera: For camera-based features
  • Photo Library: For saving and loading photos
  • Notifications (optional): For reminders

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains locally on your device, you benefit from the security mechanisms of your iOS device, including encryption and Face ID/Touch ID.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data stored with us that you would need to access, correct, or delete. All data is exclusively on your device.
